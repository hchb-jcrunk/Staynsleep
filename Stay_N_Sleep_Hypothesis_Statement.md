# Stay N’ Sleep – Hypothesis Statement

## Step 1: Identify Your User
- Leisure travellers – Wes  
- First-time users – Wes  
- Repeat users – Wes  
- Business travelers / Corporate clients – Jacob Crunk  
- Hosts – Wes  
- Corporations / Corporate travel orgs – Wes  

## Step 2: State the Need or Opportunity
- Meaningful loyalty program that rewards repeat bookings  
- Financial saving incentives  
- Single service for business and leisure travel  
- Personal incentives resulting from business travel  
- Mixing business and pleasure travel  
- Cheaper travel costs for businesses  
- Stress-free tools and booking  
- Loyalty and retention  

## Step 3: Propose a Solution
- Rewards program that rewards booking all travel experiences, regardless of type.

## Step 4: Define the Outcome
- Number of users enrolled in the loyalty/rewards program  
- Number of experiences booked  
- Conversion of travelers from leisure to business and vice versa  
- Percentage of users that book both types of travel  
- Retention by number of trips booked through Stay N Sleep  
- Loyalty points accumulated (aggregate)  

## Step 5: Include Reasoning or Assumptions
- Users have requested this for several months  
- Business travelers are the fastest-growing segment  
- Opportunity to incentivize personal travel bookings  
- **Hypothesis:**  
  > Travelers want to be rewarded for both business and leisure travel.  
  >  
  > A strong loyalty program will incentivize users to book all travel through Stay N Sleep.  
  >  
  > Success = increase in users booking both types of trips and accumulated loyalty points.  
  >  
  > Evidence: repeated user requests over the past 6 months.
