# Stay N’ Sleep – Business Model Canvas

## Key Partners
- Property hosts & local experience providers  
- B2B corporate travel firms & HR departments  
- Payment & fintech partners  
- Technology vendors (CRM, analytics, loyalty integration)  

## Key Activities
- Market research, persona definition, journey mapping  
- Identify rewards vendors  
- Design loyalty program and key features  

## Value Proposition
- Personalized recommendations  
- Rewards redemption for travel/trips (financial benefits)  
- Reward tiers for travelers, hosts, and corporate accounts  
- Higher satisfaction for business travelers by integrating leisure experiences  
- Managing business travel efficiently  
- Access to premium bookings  
- Hosts gain access to rewards program users  
- Unified ecosystem for experience and travel purchases  

## Customer Relationships
- Emphasis on customer engagement, communication, and personalization  

## Customer Segments
- Leisure travelers  
- First-time users  
- Repeat users  
- Business travelers / corporate clients  
- Hosts  
- Corporate travel orgs  

## Channels
- Existing dashboard  
- Website & mobile app  
- Emails and notifications  
- B2B client satisfaction & services  

## Key Resources
- Technology platform (mobile app, website, loyalty engine)  
- Data infrastructure (analytics, segmentation)  
- Brand and community reputation  
- Partnership network (hosts, corporate partners, financial institutions)  

## Cost Structure
- Product and tech development  
- Marketing  
- Retention  

## Revenue Streams
- Transaction fees from bookings  
- Partner advertising and cross-promotions  
- Add-on experiences and premium upgrades
